# Hydration guidance (daily + training timing)
Because recent load is elevated [`load.last7.moving_time_hours`] and recovery signals are limited by missing sleep data [`load.last7.sleep_days_with_data`], keep hydration consistent:

**Daily baseline**
- Rough target: **2.0–2.5 L/day**, plus what you lose in training (adjust for heat/sweat).

**Pre-session**
- 400–600 mL water in the 2 hours before; add a pinch of salt/electrolyte if you’re a salty sweater.

**During**
- <60 min easy: water is fine.
- 60–120 min: **500–750 mL/hour**, add electrolytes.
- 2+ hours: **500–900 mL/hour** + electrolytes; pair with carbs (drink mix helps).

**Post**
- 500–750 mL over the next hour + a meal/snack containing sodium (soup, salted carbs) to help rehydrate.
